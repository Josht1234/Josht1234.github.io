Magnetar
- Has the most powerful magnetic fields in the universe
- Magnetic fields measure at one million billion Gauss the Sun only measures at 5 Gauss
- We currently only know about 10 magnetars in the Milky Way Galaxy
- They are only formed during a supernova, but only one in ten supernova explosions results in a magnetar
- Magnetar's can destroy your credit card from hundreds of millions of miles away don't worry though the nearest one is around 50,000 light-years away
- Magnetars and any type of neutron star spins very rapidly the fastest one rotates a little over 700 times per second
Supernova
- A star must be above 8 times solar mass to even go supernova, which mines our Sun well not go supernova
- Supernova's are stars dieing with a bang
- Most supernovas shine 10 billion times brighter then the sun, and release 1044 joules, as much as the sun will produce in its 10 billion year lifetime
